How To Vote

Every month, you are given 5 meeples . These are your voting tokens. Each vote costs 1 meeple . Your meeples are tracked by a user name. You can change your vote as many times as you want, and allocate them to as many different games as you want.

If you vote, and then don't show up, and you do that several times, eventually your account will be deleted. Voting closes at 5pm on Game Night to ensure that people know what games to bring in time to get to the event. The vote resets for the next Game Night the day after the event.
